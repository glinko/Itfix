# ITfix Master Specification
Version: 1.0

## Project Overview
ITfix is a local technology services business operating in Broward County and Palm Beach County, Florida.
The website serves as the primary lead-generation platform for residential and small-business customers seeking technology support, troubleshooting, installation, and infrastructure services.

**Primary Objective:**
Generate qualified service leads

**Secondary Objectives:**
- Build trust
- Demonstrate technical expertise
- Support local SEO
- Create recurring customer relationships
- Establish a scalable technology services brand
