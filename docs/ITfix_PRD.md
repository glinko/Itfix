# ITfix Product Requirements Document

Version: 1.0

---

# Product Vision

ITfix is a lead-generation and customer acquisition platform for a local technology services business operating in South Florida.

The platform must:

- Explain services
- Establish trust
- Generate leads
- Support local SEO
- Scale into a complete customer management platform

---

# Business Goals

Primary Goal:

Generate qualified service requests.

Success Metric:

- Phone calls
- SMS messages
- Quote requests

Secondary Goals:

- Improve local search visibility
- Increase conversion rate
- Build customer trust
- Create recurring customers

---

# User Types

## Residential Customer

Needs help with:

- Computer issues
- Wi-Fi problems
- Smart home devices
- Cameras
- Printers
- Internet issues

Primary concern:

"I need this fixed quickly."

---

## Small Business Customer

Needs help with:

- Networks
- Workstations
- Cameras
- Internet reliability
- Infrastructure

Primary concern:

"I need my business running."

---

# User Journey

## Discovery

User finds website through:

- Google Search
- Google Maps
- Referral
- Social media

---

## Evaluation

User reviews:

- Services
- Pricing
- Service area
- FAQ

---

## Contact

User:

- Calls
- Sends SMS
- Requests quote

---

## Conversion

Lead is entered into CRM.

---

# Functional Requirements

## Homepage

Must contain:

- Hero
- Services
- Common Problems
- Pricing
- Service Area
- FAQ
- Contact CTA

---

## Services

Each service page must contain:

- Description
- Common symptoms
- Typical solutions
- Pricing information
- CTA

---

## Contact Forms

Required fields:

- Name
- Phone
- Email
- City
- Service Type
- Description

Optional:

- Photos
- Preferred contact method

---

## Lead Management

All submissions must:

- Be stored
- Generate notification
- Support CRM integration

---

# Mobile Requirements

Site must be optimized for:

- iPhone
- Android

Priority:

Mobile-first.
