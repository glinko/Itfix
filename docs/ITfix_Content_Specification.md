# Why Choose ITfix

Key Message:

Most technology problems are connected.

A slow computer may be caused by network issues.

A camera problem may be caused by Wi-Fi.

A smart home issue may be caused by poor infrastructure.

ITfix looks at the whole system instead of only one device.
