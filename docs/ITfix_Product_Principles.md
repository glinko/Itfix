# ITfix Product Principles

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document guides decision-making when specifications are incomplete.

When uncertainty exists, follow these principles.

---

# Principle 1

Mobile First

Most customers will arrive from mobile devices.

Every feature should be optimized for phones before desktop.

---

# Principle 2

Lead Generation Over Decoration

The website exists to generate leads.

Features that improve conversions have priority over cosmetic improvements.

---

# Principle 3

Speed Matters

A fast simple solution is usually better than a slow complex solution.

Prefer simplicity.

---

# Principle 4

Trust First

Customers buy trust before they buy services.

Every page should increase confidence.

---

# Principle 5

Clarity Over Cleverness

Visitors should immediately understand:

- What ITfix does
- Who ITfix serves
- How much it costs
- How to contact ITfix

Avoid clever marketing language.

---

# Principle 6

One Click To Contact

The user should never struggle to contact ITfix.

Contact actions should always be visible.

---

# Principle 7

Local Business First

Every decision should improve local visibility and local customer acquisition.

---

# Principle 8

SEO Is A Product Feature

SEO is not an afterthought.

SEO requirements must be considered during development.

---

# Principle 9

Reusable Architecture

Build once.

Reuse many times.

Avoid one-off solutions.

---

# Principle 10

Human-Friendly Technology

Technology should reduce customer stress.

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

---

# Principle 11

Documentation Is Part Of The Product

If it is not documented, it is not finished.

---

# Principle 12

Future Expansion

Every architectural decision should consider:

- Additional technicians
- CRM integration
- Customer portal
- AI automation
- Business growth

---

# Final Rule

When faced with multiple valid solutions:

Choose the option that is:

- Simpler
- Faster
- Easier to maintain
- Easier to scale
- Better for customers
